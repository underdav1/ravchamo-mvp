If you are looking for the best brunch in Vake, **Boulangerie Breton** (ბულანჟერია ბრეტონი) on Chavchavadze Avenue needs to be at the top of your list.

I stopped by today for a casual Sunday brunch and ordered their chicken sandwich, which comes served with a generous side of perfectly crispy french fries. I can honestly say this easily ranks in the top 3 best sandwiches in Vake.

## What makes it stand out

- **The bread:** being a true French-style boulangerie, the bread is exceptionally fresh with the perfect crust and texture.
- **The filling:** it's loaded with tender, flavorful chicken, melted cheese, crisp iceberg lettuce, and a brilliant signature sauce that ties it all together beautifully.

![Inside Boulangerie Breton on Chavchavadze](/blog/breton/interior.jpg)

Beyond the savory menu, the environment here is incredibly friendly, welcoming, and relaxed — making it an ideal spot to work, read, or catch up with friends. While I didn't get sweets today, I have had their carrot cake before, and it is a must-try if you have a sweet tooth.

![Boulangerie Breton exterior on Chavchavadze](/blog/breton/exterior.jpg)

Whether you're a local or just searching for a cozy cafe in Vake with top-tier food, **Boulangerie Breton delivers**. Highly recommended.

## Visit

**Address:** 49 E Ilia Chavchavadze Avenue, Tbilisi (Vake)
**Good for:** brunch, work-from-cafe, sandwiches, pastries, coffee.
